I am a fourth year PhD candidate at Cornell Tech advised by Professor Wendy Ju in the Information Science Department. I am also a doctoral fellow at the Digital Life Initiative (DLI) and a member of the Interaction Research Lab (IRL). I received my BA in Computer Science from Hunter College.

My research uses fine-grained behavioral analysis of human interactions to inform the design of context-aware digital assistance systems. By modeling human behaviors through multimodal signals, I investigate when and how technological systems should collaborate or provide support. I combine qualitative methods with system prototyping to translate insights from human behavior into the design of effective human-AI interactions.
